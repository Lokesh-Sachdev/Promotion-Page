Why is there a membership ?

It is our mission to service a smaller community of wireless mobile internet users with the best communication and service as possible. No. We are looking for responsible data users. We do not want users that download large amounts of torrents, abuse streaming limits, use TB's of data per month, game several hours a day or would be considered "couch potatoes".
